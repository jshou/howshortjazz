how short jazz

Andy and Josh met as music students at the University of Washington and have been playing together ever since. Today, they play and sing jazz standards and popular songs from the 1930s and 1940s. They can be found playing regularly at venues around Seattle.

Joshua Hou piano, accordion, vocals
Andy Short guitar, vocals
